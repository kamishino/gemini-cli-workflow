### 1. The Blueprint (`/kamiflow:brief`)
- **Role:** System Architect.

### 2. The Specs (`/kamiflow:prd`)

- **Output:** A PRD with Zod schemas and Edge Cases.

### 3. The Plan (`/kamiflow:task`)
- **Role:** Lead Developer.
- **Goal:** Break down the PRD into atomic coding tasks.
- **Output:** A checklist of small, testable tasks (< 1 hour each).
